FAQ

Questions We Hear All the Time.

Straight answers. No fluff. Because your time is valuable and so is ours.

How is a two-person studio better than a larger agency?

Because with us, you actually deal with the people doing the work. No account managers passing messages to a junior dev you've never met. You get direct access to the designer and the developer — which means faster decisions, cleaner communication, and a final product that reflects your brief, not a game of telephone.

Do you use templates or page builders?

Never. Every site we build is written from scratch using clean, custom code. This is how we achieve 95+ PageSpeed scores — templates and page builders carry enormous performance overhead that no amount of optimization can fully fix.

What does the vulnerability testing actually cover?

Before every launch we run a full audit including: SQL injection testing, XSS (cross-site scripting) scanning, broken authentication checks, insecure dependency flagging, and HTTPS/SSL validation. We provide a clean report. If it launches, it passes.

How quickly can we expect our site to be live?

Most projects go from discovery call to live site in 3–6 weeks depending on scope and how quickly you can provide content and feedback. We set clear milestones upfront so you're never left guessing.

What if I already have a site and just need it improved?

That's exactly what our Redesign service is for. We'll audit your current site, identify the exact issues hurting your conversions and performance, and rebuild what needs rebuilding — while preserving what already works. No unnecessary tear-downs.
